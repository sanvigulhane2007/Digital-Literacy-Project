STAY SAFETY ONLINE - PREVENTION CHECKLIST


1) Use strong and unique passwords.
2) Never share OTP or banking details with anyone.
3) Avoid clicking unknown or suspicious links.
4) Enable two-factor authentication on accounts.
5) Do not scan unknown QR codes for payments.
6) Always verify payment requests before sending money.
7) Keep your devices and apps updated.
8) Report cybercrime immediately through cybercrime.gov.in or helpline 1930.
