## Project Overview
This repository contains my Digital Literacy Portfolio Project completed as part of the CSE0001 Digital Literacy course. The project aims to help students understand the importance of digital literacy in academics and everyday life. It includes activities related to digital tools, professional online presence, safe internet practices, communication etiquette, and cybercrime awareness.

Through this project, I explored various digital platforms and learned how they can be used for learning, collaboration, and professional development.

---



## Task Summary

### Task 1 – Digital Literacy Awareness Infographic
Created a one-page infographic explaining the concept of digital literacy and its importance for students. The infographic covers topics such as useful digital tools, safe internet practices, professional online presence, and email etiquette.

---

### Task 2 – Student Digital Portfolio
Created professional accounts on platforms such as GitHub, LinkedIn, and Kaggle to build a digital portfolio. These platforms allow students to showcase their projects, skills, and educational achievements.

---

### Task 3 – Coding and Collaboration Platforms
Explored coding practice platforms by solving a beginner challenge on HackerRank. Also created a Digital Literacy Awareness Quiz using Google Forms to demonstrate the use of online collaboration tools.

### Task 4 – Professional Email and Social Media Etiquette
Prepared two professional email drafts: one requesting an assignment extension and another expressing interest in a summer internship opportunity. Also created a checklist of social media do’s and don’ts to promote responsible digital communication.

---

### Task 5 – Cybercrime Awareness
Researched online payment fraud as a cybercrime case study and created a prevention checklist to help students stay safe online.
